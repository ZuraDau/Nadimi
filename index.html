Our Story

A wine born from the spirit of gathering

NADIMI is the house wine of Restaurant Symposium, in the vibrant seaside city of Batumi, Georgia. The brand was created to embody the ancient ritual of the symposium — the Greek and Georgian tradition of coming together over food, wine, and meaningful conversation.

Designed with an illustrated identity that bridges ancient culture and contemporary craft, each label tells the story of a place, a moment, and a toast shared among friends.

The Place

Born in Batumi, on the shore of the Black Sea

Batumi is a city of contrasts — ancient and modern, European and Caucasian, mountainous and maritime. It is here, among the palms and the cobblestones, that NADIMI found its voice.

🌊

Black Sea Coast

The humid, subtropical climate of Adjara shapes a unique terroir — wild, intense, and unlike anywhere else in Georgia.

🏛️

Ancient Culture

Georgia is home to one of the world's oldest wine cultures — 8,000 years of unbroken tradition, from qvevri clay jars to the modern glass.

🍇

Native Varieties

Over 500 indigenous grape varieties grow in Georgia. NADIMI celebrates the finest of them with honesty and craft.
